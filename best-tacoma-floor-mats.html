How to Choose Tacoma Floor Mats

  • All-weather vs carpet: If you use your Tacoma off-road or in bad weather, all-weather liners are a must. Carpet mats are fine for pavement-only trucks.
  • Sidewall height: Higher sidewalls = more protection. WeatherTech leads here.
  • Material: TPE (WeatherTech) is stiff but durable. Rubber (Husky) is softer. Multi-layer (TuxMat) looks best.
  • Cab type: Make sure you're buying for the right cab — Access Cab and Double Cab have different rear floor areas.
  • Year: 2nd gen (2005–2015) and 3rd gen (2016–2023) need different mats.

WeatherTech FloorLiners are our top pick. They offer laser-measured fit, high sidewalls, and excellent protection against mud, water, and debris. Made in the USA with a lifetime warranty.
